Detour Blackman/Summit

Effective: 3/16/2014

Effective immediately (March 16, 2014) the Blackman/Summit Route will take a detour due to poor road conditions.
Starting at Airport Rd and Lawrence Ave., the bus will turn right onto Michigan Ave. instead of turning left. Thr Route will then turn left on Oak Grove, then right on Main St., and left on Robinson Rd. to complete the rest of the route. The Oaks Apartment Complex and Washington Way will not be served. The residents of the Commons Apartment Complex will have to catch the bus on Main Street. They will need to signal to the driver to stop, due to no bus sign.
